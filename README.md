# Amazon-lipstick-sale-analysis
This project analyzes lipstick product data from online cosmetic platforms to study pricing trends and customer preferences. It explores selling price, discounts, ratings, shade intensity, and price categories using visual analysis and statistical tests to support better pricing strategies and purchase decisions.
his project analyzes lipstick product data collected from online cosmetic platforms to understand pricing patterns and customer preferences. The analysis focuses on selling price, MRP, discounts, ratings, shade intensity, and price categories.

# Objectives

Analyze lipstick pricing across different categories
Study customer ratings and discount impact
Identify popular shade intensity preferences
Compare budget, mid-range, premium, and luxury products

# Tools & Technologies

Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Seaborn
SciPy

# Analysis Performed

Univariate, bivariate, and multivariate analysis
Visualizations: histograms, box plots, bar charts, pie charts, scatter plots, and line charts
Hypothesis testing using ANOVA, t-test, and Chi-Square test

# Key Insights
Selling prices vary significantly across price categories
Customer ratings are similar across all price ranges
Light shade lipsticks are the most preferred
Higher discounts do not strongly affect customer ratings

# Conclusion

The project provides useful insights into lipstick pricing and customer behavior. These findings can help brands improve pricing strategies and assist customers in choosing value-for-money products.
